Who We Are

India's Integrated Talent & AI Support Ecosystem

Qanat Global Solutions (QGS) is a multi-vertical organization headquartered in India, specializing in AI data services, B2B demand generation, lead generation, data extraction, digital marketing, and voice projects.

We are building India's most integrated Talent & AI support ecosystem — one that combines trained human workforces with data infrastructure, channel intelligence, and digital growth capabilities to serve clients globally.

Our philosophy is simple: technology is powerful, but human intelligence makes it meaningful. Every dataset we produce, every lead we deliver, and every campaign we run is touched by real people committed to quality.

Empowering Talent — We build careers and capabilities in our workforce, not just outputs.

Enabling Growth — We measure our success by our clients' pipeline, not our own metrics.

Delivering Intelligence — We combine human expertise with smart processes to deliver insight, not just data.
